Privacy Policy

GlowUp — AI-Powered Appearance Improvement Coach

Effective Date: April 1, 2026

Introduction

GlowUp ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our iOS mobile application and related services.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our app.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address (for authentication and account management)
  • Account credentials through Apple Sign-In

Face Images and Facial Data

  • Photographs of your face that you voluntarily submit for AI analysis
  • AI-generated facial feature measurements and improvement scores derived from your photos
  • Jawline, skin, hair, and symmetry analysis results

Usage Data

  • AI face scan history and improvement scores over time
  • Interaction logs with the app's features (mewing lab, exercises, hairstyle recommendations)
  • Streak and progress tracking data
  • Chat messages with our AI coaching assistant

Subscription Information

  • Subscription status and purchase history (managed by RevenueCat)

2. Facial Data Handling and AI Processing Disclosure

Your facial data is sensitive, and we treat it with the highest level of care.

  • Face images you submit are processed by our AI systems to generate appearance analysis scores, improvement suggestions, and coaching recommendations.
  • Facial analysis is performed using third-party AI services (see Section 3 below). Your images may be transmitted to these services solely for the purpose of generating your analysis results.
  • We do not use your face images or facial data for facial recognition, identity verification, or any purpose other than providing you with appearance improvement suggestions.
  • We do not sell, license, or share your face images or facial analysis data with advertisers or data brokers.
  • You may request deletion of all your facial data at any time (see Section 5).

Important: GlowUp provides AI-generated suggestions for informational and entertainment purposes only. Results are not guaranteed and should not be considered medical or professional advice.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain app functionality and services
  • To authenticate your identity and secure your account
  • To perform AI-powered face scans and generate improvement scores
  • To provide personalized coaching, hairstyle recommendations, and exercise guidance
  • To power our AI-driven coaching chat assistant
  • To track your progress and streaks over time
  • To manage subscriptions and process payments
  • To improve our app, AI models, services, and user experience
  • To send service-related notifications and support messages
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Third-Party Services

Supabase

We use Supabase for authentication and secure data storage. Supabase stores your account credentials, analysis history, and progress data. Supabase has its own privacy practices — please review their Privacy Policy for details.

RevenueCat

We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions and process in-app purchases. RevenueCat handles payment information and subscription status. Please review RevenueCat's Privacy Policy for details on their data practices.

Apple Sign-In

We use Apple Sign-In for authentication. Apple does not share your email with us unless you allow it. Please review Apple's Privacy Policy for details.

OpenAI (Third-Party AI Service)

We use OpenAI's API (specifically GPT-4.1-mini with vision capabilities) to power our AI facial analysis and coaching chat assistant. When you use the face scan feature, your face photo is transmitted as a base64-encoded image to OpenAI's API through our secure server to generate your appearance analysis scores and improvement recommendations.

Face Data Shared with OpenAI

  • What data is shared: Your face photo (base64-encoded image) and your selected gender are sent to OpenAI's API when you initiate a face scan.
  • Purpose: OpenAI processes this data solely to generate your facial analysis scores, category ratings, and personalized improvement recommendations.
  • Storage by OpenAI: Per OpenAI's API data usage policy, API inputs and outputs are not used to train OpenAI's models and are not stored permanently. OpenAI may retain API data for up to 30 days for abuse and misuse monitoring purposes, after which it is automatically deleted. OpenAI does not use your face data for any purpose other than processing your API request and security monitoring.
  • Why data is retained for 30 days: OpenAI retains API data temporarily to detect abuse, prevent misuse, and ensure platform safety. This retention is required by OpenAI's operational policies and cannot be shortened by GlowUp.
  • OpenAI's privacy practices: OpenAI is committed to data protection and does not sell API user data. For full details, please review OpenAI's Privacy Policy and API Data Usage Policy.

Chat Data Shared with OpenAI

When you use the AI coaching chat feature, your chat messages along with relevant profile data (name, gender, age range, goals) are sent to OpenAI's API to generate personalized coaching responses. The same data retention policies described above apply to chat data.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We only share information with third-party service providers as necessary to operate the app and provide its services (Supabase, RevenueCat, and OpenAI).

We may disclose your information if required by law, to protect our legal rights, or to respond to lawful government requests.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data as long as your account is active and necessary to provide services. If you request account deletion, we will remove your personal information — including all face images and facial analysis data — from our systems within 30 days, except where required by law to retain records.

You can request deletion of your account and associated data through the app's Settings screen or by contacting us at glowupaiapp@gmail.com.

7. Children's Privacy

GlowUp is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete such information and terminate the child's account.

8. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encryption and secure data storage through Supabase. Face images are transmitted over encrypted connections. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data, including any stored face images and analysis results
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data, including all facial data
  • Opt-out of certain data uses

To exercise these rights, please contact us at glowupaiapp@gmail.com.
